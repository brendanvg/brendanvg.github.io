Shabbat! A Dinner Party

The Quintessential Jewish Party

Shabbat dinner has been bringing the challah and wine for over two thousand years. Whether you know the blessings or not, you can throw an awesome Shabbes dinner – all you need is a bunch of Jews, some roast chicken (or tacos), and a couple bottles of Manischewitz. Everyone loves Shabbat dinner – so give the people what they want!

Follow this checklist and make it happen!

Choose a date

Choose a location

Make a guest list (and actually invite folks)

Cook a “traditional” meal (You know, chicken, potatoes, etc.)OR order tacos or pizza or Vietnamese pho... get Lots of Wine! Lots of Challah...and a couple candles

Determine who might know how to “bless” the above ritual items (could be guest or host...if no one knows, then just light, drink, and eat)

Deck your apartment out real nice: Tablecloths, mood lighting, Jewish music from Spotify…you know, the works

As guests arrive, serve appetizers

Do the Shabbat table rituals – in a way that’s comfortable for you

Consider deepening conversation by asking a “Jewish Question” that’s been on your mind
–or choose from these “100 Prompts, Provocations, and Situations for Jewish Growth”

Eat something sweet

Make a Lechayim!

Use your phone to take a picture of all receipts and email these pics to the JThing staff – they’ll send out a reimbursement check ASAP
