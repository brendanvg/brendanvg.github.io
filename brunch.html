Nothing says, “Life is good” more than a chilled-out Sunday brunch. It’s a chance to catch up with friends, chat about the latest news, and recover from the previous evening’s adventures. And it’s easy to make a Sunday brunch kinda Jewish – all it takes is a handful of Jews and one good Jewish question. So what’s been on your mind?

Follow this checklist and make it happen!

Find a Sunday morning that works well with some friends

Make a guest list (and actually invite folks)

– or just buy a bunch of bagels, shmear, and lox Mimosas are optional

Put on some music

Have everyone over Nosh

Think about a “Jewish question” you’d like to ask everyone,
