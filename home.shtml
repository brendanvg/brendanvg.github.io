Executive Coaching Board Directors

People who make things happen need a coach who knows what it is to walk through the practical issues and inner journey of the transitions they are facing. As a former corporate CEO and non-profit executive director, David Lesser understands how to use the pressures and opportunities of business life to further the deepest ranges of your life purpose.

David Lesser and Associates whole being coaching brings together the basic tools of leadership development, emotional dynamics, spiritual practice and body-energy work to walk with you through your most crucial transitions.



David serves as an independent director on the board of a number of major growing businesses and non-profit organizations. He and his team understand how to turn around difficult boardroom dynamics and make the top level of the corporation fertile with creativity.
